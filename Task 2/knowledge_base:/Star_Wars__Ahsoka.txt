Disney+
in
2023

Dave Filoni

Rosario Dawson
as
Ahsoka Tano

9 ABY
after Ahsoka Tano's appearance in
The Mandalorian
season two

Imperial
Grand Admiral
Thrawn
started in the epilogue of the animated series
Star Wars Rebels

was announced on
January 9

2024
and the series' story will be concluded by an
upcoming untitled film
directed by Filoni.
