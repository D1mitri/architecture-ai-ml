Development
[
]
During a presentation at
Disney
Investor Day
2020
,
Lucasfilm
President
Kathleen Kennedy
announced that interconnected stories between series such as
Star Wars: The Mandalorian
and
Ahsoka
would cross over and culminate in a "climactic story event."
[
5
]
On
April 7
,
2023
, at a panel at
Celebration Europe
, the project was revealed to be a theatrical film helmed by director
Dave Filoni
. A news post on
StarWars.com
the same day confirmed that the film would "close out" the stories started in series like
The Mandalorian
.
[
1
]
In an interview with
IGN
at the convention, Kennedy discussed how the genesis for the film had started over a decade prior and evolved organically into the current iteration of the project. She also revealed that Filoni would write the film.
[
3
]
Also at Celebration, Filoni was interviewed by
Variety
and revealed that the film would center around a conflict between the
New Republic
and
Imperial Remnants
.
[
4
]
When interviewed by
Empire
on
May 5
, Filoni discussed how he wants the film to deal with moments that "define an era," in this case the space between
Star Wars
: Episode VI
Return of the Jedi
and
Star Wars
: Episode VII
The Force Awakens
.
